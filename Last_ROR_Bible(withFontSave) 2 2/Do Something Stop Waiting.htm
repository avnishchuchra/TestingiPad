PASTOR CHRIS

DO SOMETHING;
STOP WAITING

When the children of Israel left Egypt, they came to the Red Sea and their adversaries, the Egyptians, were fast advancing toward them from behind. Then Moses cried out to God for help but God said, "Moses, why do you cry to Me. Tell the people to go forward!" (Exodus 14:15-16). God's response is most remarkable because Moses had thought it was God's responsibility, but God said, "No, you do something; it's your responsibility!" We oftentimes blame God for what isn't His responsibility. God has already done everything He needed to do. Everything else is entirely up to us. God doesn't want you to wait any longer; He's given you His Word and shown you the Kingdom principle, so DO IT! It's the doer of the Word that's blessed in his deeds (James 1:25). There was a severe famine in Israel and the king had no solution, so he sent an SOS message to Elisha, a prophet of God. Prior to that, Elisha had being waiting on God to transform the economy of the country, but nothing was happening. Why? Because God was waiting on him to do something! In 2 Kings 7:1, Elisha declared prosperity upon the land. And within twenty-four hours from the time he spoke, the economy of the nation was transformed. The knowledge of God's Word is so important. You can't live victoriously in Christ in this life without it. If you're just sitting there, twiddling your thumbs and expecting something good to turn up, your bills will continue to pile up.

"And the LORD said unto Moses, Wherefore criest thou unto me? speak unto the children of Israel, that they go forward" (Exodus 14:15).

Rather, make good things happen. Receive God's direction today and go right ahead to do whatever He's told you.
