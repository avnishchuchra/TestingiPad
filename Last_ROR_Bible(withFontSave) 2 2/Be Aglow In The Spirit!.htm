PASTOR ANITA

BE AGLOW IN THE SPIRIT!

There're folks who for the first few months, even years after they were born again were on fire for the Lord! They were in every prayer meeting; always available for evangelism, and the first to volunteer for a new project; they prophesied in every meeting because they were so aglow in the Spirit. However, as time went on, some of such folks began to wane in zeal; their enthusiasm for the things of the Spirit began to dissipate. The hitherto spiritual shine and excitement they once manifested gradually declined! They've become too familiar with God. Now, they consider the fervent zeal of younger believers in their midst as "youthful exuberance." How sad! Never allow the fire of God in your spirit wane or dissipate. You have the responsibility to maintain your zeal for God to a boiling point-be aglow in the Spirit. Whether you've been born again for two months or twenty years, you ought to maintain your zeal for the Lord; have that child-like attitude in following the Master. Give His Word first place in your life, and have high regard for spiritual things. So if before now, it looked as though that spiritual excitement you once knew when you first believed had gradually ebbed away, then it's time to remind yourself of those things that once stirred your spirit! Get back to speaking in tongues and winning souls; get back to loving God's Word and studying it voraciously. There's always something fresh and new in God, and if you keep your spirit aglow doing the Lord's work, there'll be no dull moment in your life.

"Not slothful in business; fervent in spirit; serving the Lord" (Romans 12:11).

Your life will be full of the anointing and power of the Holy Ghost, and you'll only know the upward and forward life.
