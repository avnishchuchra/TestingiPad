PASTOR CHRIS

"JESUS" IS THE NAME
ABOVE ALL NAMES

Philippians 2:9-10 is a sovereign declaration of Deity: At the mention of the Name of Jesus, every knee is compelled to bow. The Name of Jesus has power over all things, and over all beings; whether they be in heaven, on earth, or in hell. Kenneth Hagin (of blessed memory) tells a story of how as a young boy, he died and went to hell. When he got to the gates of hell, a beastly-looking creature took a hold of him to lead him into hell. Suddenly, he said, he heard a voice from far above him. He didn't understand what the voice said, but he recalled that when the voice spoke, all of hell shook and the being let him go. He didn't know who it was that spoke then, but he found out it was none other than the Lord Jesus. Jesus has power and authority over everything that exists. That's why Peter could say to a man who was impotent in his feet and had never walked from birth: "In the name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth rise up and walk" (Acts 3:6), and the man leaped and walked. The Name of Jesus is higher than the name of any sickness! As long as that sickness has a name, be it HIV, cancer, pneumonia, tuberculosis- whatever it is, it's subject to the Name of Jesus. Learn to call the Name of Jesus with confidence and with revelation; live in that Name, for it's the Name vested with all power.

"Wherefore God also hath highly exalted him, and given him a name which is above every name: That at the name of Jesus every knee should bow, of things in heaven, and things in earth, and things under the earth" (Philippians 2:9-10).

It's higher, greater and more powerful than any other name that exists. There's power in that Name. It's the Name above every name.
