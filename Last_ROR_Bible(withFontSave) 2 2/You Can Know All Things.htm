PASTOR CHRIS

YOU CAN KNOW ALL THINGS

The Bible says all of the treasures of wisdom and knowledge are hidden in Christ, and we know that Christ in the context of this Scripture doesn't refer to Jesus alone: "we, being many, are one body in Christ, and every one members one of another" (Romans 12:5). In other words, Christ refers to the Church, of which Jesus is the Head and we are His body. Thus, if all the treasures of wisdom and knowledge are hid in Christ, that means nothing ought to be beyond your scope of knowledge as a child of God. No wonder the Bible declares that we have an anointing from the Holy One and we know all things (1 John 2:20). That anointing dwells in your spirit, which is the real you. That's where the light of God shines and it's through your spirit that you can draw from the boundless treasure-house of God's knowledge. Let me give you a practical example: Maybe you misplaced something and had tried fruitlessly to remember where you kept it. In your mind, there's no clue as to where and how to find it. But do you know that your spirit doesn't forget? If you would activate your spirit by speaking in tongues, your spirit will begin to function as a searchlight! Before long, your spirit will send signals to your mind and you'll know where that thing is.

"That their hearts might be comforted, being knit together in love, and unto all riches of the full assurance of understanding, to the acknowledgement of the mystery of God, and of the Father, and of Christ; In whom are hid all the treasures of wisdom and knowledge" (Colossians 2:2-3).

Someone may ask, "Does this really work?" Emphatically yes! No child of God should be in the dark about anything. Should there be anything you need knowledge about? Through the agency of the Holy Spirit, you can access knowledge of any topic in the world.
