PASTOR CHRIS

GET RID OF PRIDE

Some people have the erroneous idea that you have to be rich to be proud. However, the rich and successful people aren't always proud. In most cases, true success and riches are the expression of humility. The Bible says, "But he giveth more grace. Wherefore he saith, God resisteth the proud, but giveth grace unto the humble"(James 4:6). So for the rich man to be exalted thus, it means he must have been humble at some point in his life. Forget about those who may have used the wrong means to attain some level of status in the society. I'm talking about those who through their hard work or some blessing of favour have found themselves journeying successfully on the ladder of wealth or prominence. The spiritual principle is that a man first must be humble to get into the position of greatness. Pride will impede your prosperity. Saul, the king of Israel, started out as a humble man. The Lord chose him as king over His people, Israel, because of his humility (1 Samuel 15:17). But when he became proud and impudent a few years into his reign in Israel, the Lord took the kingship away from him and his household, and gave it to David, a lowly shepherd boy. Humble people impress God. No wonder the Bible says that God exalts the humble; no wonder a timid fisherman like Peter became a great apostle; no wonder a harlot like Rahab became a forebear of the Lord Jesus ; no wonder an injurious personality like Saul became the great apostle Paul.

"A man's pride shall bring him low: but honour shall uphold the humble in spirit" (Proverbs 29:23).

How does God see you? Make up your mind to walk in humility today.
