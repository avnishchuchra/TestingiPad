PASTOR ANITA

BLESSED BEYOND MEASURE

When God blesses a man, he remains a blessed man. It's impossible for him to be cursed anymore. Moreover, that blessing can't be reversed. So a Christian has no business trying to get rid of any curse, because he's blessed. He was born into God's blessing (Ephesians 1:3). You're a new creation, a new species that never existed before (2 Corinthians 5:17). You have no past but a glorious future. You're the reflection of God's beauty. The situation in your life that simulates the influence of a curse is not a curse at all but a mentality or a mindset. So you see, ignorance is the reason for your predicaments and challenges (Hosea 4:6). To change your mentality isn't a difficult task. All you need is to focus your attention on the Word of God. It'll wash your mind of the wrong information and give you a new mentality (Romans 12:2). Some ministers pride themselves on being able to detect and remove curses. Such 'spiritual detectives' are only out to deceive because there are no valid scriptural parameters for detecting whether or not a man is under the influence of curses. If you listen to such people, you'll soon lose the beauty of what God has done in your life. You were translated from the kingdom of darkness into the Kingdom of light (Colossians 1:13). You've been called to inherit a blessing (1 Peter 3:9). God chose and called you into a blessing (John 15:16). Therefore, discover the worth of the price Jesus paid for you; think about the beauty of God's Word; talk about the great things God is doing in your life.

"...the LORD thy God would not hearken unto Balaam; but the Lord thy God turned the curse into a blessing unto thee, because the Lord thy God loved thee" (Deuteronomy 23:5).

Stop referring to the failures of the past. Leave your past behind and keep marching on to your glorious future in Christ Jesus.
