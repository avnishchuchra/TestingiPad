PASTOR CHRIS

SEE THE INVISIBLE, BELIEVE THE IMPOSSIBLE

Our God is a loving God! He doesn't take delight in our misery. He is the Lord of the universe. The Bible says that He created all things for His own pleasure (Revelation 4:11). He desires to give you everything you want because you're His child. Your prosperity is hinged on the presence of God with you, so when you're conscious of His presence, all your struggles will be over. Joseph recognized this truth and took advantage of it (Genesis 39:2). This young man knew all along that he would be the ruler of nations and all his kinfolks would bow before him. In spite of the many troubles that came upon him, he still saw the invisible and believed the impossible. This is why he wasn't bitter with those who ill-treated him; he knew he was going somewhere. Whatever it is you desire, our God" is able to do exceeding abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that worketh in us" (Ephesians 3:20). Did you see that? The power isn't coming from above; it's inside you now! The Lord Jesus wouldn't try to negotiate you out of your desire (Matthew 21:22). Remember that God told Abraham that He gave him as far as his eyes could see (Genesis 13:14-15). If you can see it, you shall have it. He'll not only help you get a good job in that company, He can make you own it. Sounds incredible, doesn't it? But it's the truth nonetheless! Joseph was a forgotten
