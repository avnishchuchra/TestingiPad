PASTOR ANITA

EXPERIENCE THE FULLNESS OF
GOD'S BLESSING

Isn't it wonderful to know that the fullness of God's blessings belong to you? It's your set time to experience the bountiful blessings of God in overflowing measure. Somebody said, "I've been praying to God to bless me." That's the wrong prayer. God isn't going to bless you any more than He already did. He already blessed you with ALL spiritual blessings in heavenly places in Christ Jesus, not some. The question now is: Are you in Christ? For that's where the blessings are-in Christ. So if you're in Christ, you don't need to look for any blessing anywhere else, or pray to God to bless you. Simply recognize that it's your set time to enjoy the fullness of God's blessings. The Bible says you were called to inherit a blessing (1 Peter 3:9); you were born into the blessing because you're the seed of Abraham. The Lord told Abraham, "I will bless you and make you a blessing" (Genesis 12:2). This promise was to Abraham and his seed (Galatians 3:16), which means not only are you blessed, you are also a blessing. Make up your mind to live up to your calling. You're full of the blessings of God. There's no blessing in this world or in heaven that you should be asking God for, because all things are yours (1 Corinthians 3:21). You ought to be basking in superabundance! Your life should be filled with the good things of life in an overflowing measure, so that others who come in contact with you can partake of the goodness of God in your life.

"Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who hath blessed us with all spiritual blessings in heavenly places in Christ" (Ephesians 1:3).

This is the set time for that to happen.
