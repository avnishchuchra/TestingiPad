PASTOR CHRIS

THE WORLD IS WAITING FOR YOU

When God created the world, everything He made was good, and He gave man dominion over His creation. But when sin entered the world through Adam's transgression, death also came in and from that point on, everything in the world became subject to decay and regression. That's why today, everything that exists in the world, living and non-living, is crying out for liberation. Men are groaning under the weight of sickness, disease and poverty; even money is crying out to be delivered from those who use it for evil. The only hope for the world are the sons of God, and that's why Scripture declares that the whole of creation is waiting for the manifestation of the "sons" of God. The word "sons" is from the Greek word "huios" and it means the matured sons of God, those who have come of age spiritually. The matured sons of God are those who know how to dominate sickness, disease, poverty, and the forces of nature! They are the ones who command prosperity for the sake of the Kingdom, and rescue men and women from darkness. Make up your mind not to remain in the babyhood stage of Christianity. Grow up to maturity because the world is waiting for you! God wants to deliver great things to matured sons and daughters, for He knows they can handle responsibilities. He is waiting for His kids to come to that place of maturity in Christ, where they'll rise up and subdue the systems of the world.

"For the earnest expectation of the creature waiteth for the manifestation of the sons of God" (Romans 8:19).

Take your Christian growth very seriously. It's only in the position of maturity in Christ that you can take your place as the "huios" (matured son) of God and bring deliverance, healing, salvation and joy to a hurting world.
