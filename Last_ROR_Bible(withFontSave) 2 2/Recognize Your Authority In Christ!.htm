PASTOR CHRIS

RECOGNIZE YOUR AUTHORITY
IN CHRIST!

The supernatural life available to us as God's children isn't just limited to spiritual things; it extends to physical things also. Years ago, my friends and I travelled to many different places to preach. We travelled during the day and at night using an old vehicle which could suddenly develop a fault in the middle of nowhere. Each time this happened, we never responded in fear; rather we spoke to the car and said, "In the Name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth, move!" and it worked. Though young in the faith, we were able to prove that the Word works! We recognized our authority in Christ. When you find yourself in an awkward situation, the first thing to do is not to react in fear. Instead, recognize your authority in Christ. Remember that Jesus has already given you power over all the ability of the enemy, therefore there's nothing to be afraid of. If a robber points a gun to a Christian, for instance, and says, "Give me your car keys" such a fellow shouldn't become frantic, fearful and intimidated. Rather, he should ask himself, "What does the Holy Spirit want me to do in this situation?" because at that time, that's what counts. For one, the Spirit may say, "Don't give him.
" For another, He may say, "Let him have it." At that moment, what the Spirit says is the most important. You may be wondering, "How can I tell what the Spirit is saying?" That's why you ought to start speaking in tongues immediately you find yourself in an uncomfortable situation.

"Behold, I give unto you power to tread on serpents and scorpions, and over all the power of the enemy: and nothing shall by any means hurt you" (Luke 10:19).

Speaking in tongues will eliminate fear and cause faith to well up in your spirit, and you'll find yourself declaring God's Word that'll put you over in that situation. Refuse to fear. Recognize that you're a victor in Christ Jesus, and you have authority over all the power of the adversary.
