PASTOR CHRIS

YOU CAN CAST OUT DEVILS

The Lord Jesus never said signs shall follow only pastors, prophets and evangelists." Rather, He said these signs shall follow the believing ones-those who are born again! This means that every child of God can cast out devils, both the one who got born again last week and the one who's been born again for twenty years. You don't ever have to be afraid of the devil or his little demons, because the Lord Jesus has completely and finally paralyzed them! The Bible records that Jesus shook off from Himself the hostile princes and rulers and boldly displayed them as His conquests when He triumphed over them by the Cross (Colossians 2:15). Jesus' victory over the devil was for you and me. That's why He said, "In my Name, shall they cast out devils." That's another way of saying, "Kick them out in the Name of Jesus!" It's not a question of how "strong" or "spiritual" you are; the issue is that Jesus has given you His own authority. You have the power of attorney to use the Name of Jesus. Thus, when you say "Devil, get out of here in the Name of Jesus," he's got no option but to scamper away in terror! The devil is no longer a factor. You can stop him any day, any time and anywhere if he tries to play his games around you.

"And these signs shall follow them that believe; In my name shall they cast out devils..." (Mark 16:17).

No need to negotiate with him; cast him out! The devil has no right to run things in your life, or in your home, or in the lives of your loved ones. It's time to put a stop to his work by exercising your authority in Christ.
