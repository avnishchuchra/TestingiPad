PASTOR CHRIS

THE TRANSFORMING POWER OF
THE WORD

The Word of God has the ability to produce what it talks about. When it addresses a particular situation, divine power is released, thereby causing that situation to align with the Word. For example, when God was creating the world, He didn't have to roll out bulldozers and cranes from heaven. All He did was speak; He released His Word and by it created the whole world. God's Word is the solution to every challenge. It is potent and efficacious. It's the infallible universal panacea. It has inherent ability to cause changes. This is why it's imperative for you as a Christian to study and imbibe God's Word, for it has the ability to transform your life, energize you for victory, and programme you for success. As you meditate on the Word, there's a transformation, a metamorphosis, that takes place in your life; the Word produces in you what it talks about. If a poor person for example, keeps s tudying and confessing the Word regarding his inheritance and riches in Christ, his life will be compelled to go in that direction. That's the transforming ability of God's Word; it comes to you with the capacity and power to make you exactly what it says.

"And be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect, will of God" (Romans 12:2).

If you're facing any difficulty in your life today, or maybe you simply desire a change, don't despair. What you need is to get into the Word and locate the scriptures that deal with that situation and begin to meditate on them. There'll be a transformation; a positive change will be effected in your life!
