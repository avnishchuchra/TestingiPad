PASTOR ANITA

REST IN HIS LOVE

The description of our heavenly Father is "love." He is not "judgment," as many people believe. He's a loving heavenly Father. God testified to His love for you in Jeremiah 31:3. I urge you today to rest in God's love. Quit all struggles and take advantage of God's unfeigned and unconditional love for you. You may have done so many things wrong even as a Christian, but the Lord is saying to you today, "Rest in My love!" All you need to do is repent and say, "Lord, forgive me." Once you repent, He is quick to forgive, because it is His nature to forgive. The prodigal son's father killed the best calf to celebrate his son's return (Luke 15:21-23), an allegory depicting God as an ever-forgiving and ever-loving Father. He rejoices when He finds His lost child. So, if you've been out of fellowship with Him, it's time to come home. He's waiting to receive you into His everlasting arms of love. He's not wielding a big stick to strike you for your wrongs for He already struck Jesus for your sake. All He expects from you now is that you rest in His love! There is a confident assurance in us that God never looks out for our faults. Instead, He follows us with His tender mercies. Let nothing separate you from God's love, not even the sins you've committed. No matter how grievous a man's sins are, God will still forgive and forget; He receives him and establishes him in the right path.

"And we have known and believed the love that God hath to us. God is love; and he that dwelleth in love dwelleth in God, and God in him" (1 John 4:16).

Having this kind of confidence in God's love comes by acquaintance with Him. Get to know and understand the nature of your heavenly Father, and you'll be joyful at all times in His presence.
