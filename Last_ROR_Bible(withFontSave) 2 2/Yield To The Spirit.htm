PASTOR CHRIS

YIELD TO THE SPIRIT

The more yielded you are to the Spirit of God, the greater His works are manifested through you. One of the most beautiful experiences you can have is for the Holy Spirit to take over your soul; that is, to take over your mental and physical faculties. When this happens, you are literally moved by the Holy Spirit. When the Spirit takes over your soul, you will say and do things that are beyond your understanding and your life will become truly supernatural. Ezekiel had this experience and he said, "The Spirit entered into me and set me upon my feet." What an experience the man had with the Holy Spirit! He was aware that the Spirit of God was moving him thi s way and he yielded to Him. I've learnt to yield my faculties to the Spirit of God. I've had several experiences when I knew that the words I was speaking and the actions I was taking were not just me anymore, but the Spirit in action. At those times, great miracles always take place for the Holy Spirit is in control. Have you ever found yourself just crying and groaning in prayer, unable to speak? That was the Spirit in control! The Bible says that the Holy Spirit prays through us with groanings that can't be put in articulate speech (Romans 8:26). Whenever the Holy Spirit prompts you to do something from within, don't argue or struggle with it; go ahead and take the step.

"And he said unto me, Son of man, stand upon thy feet, and I will speak unto thee. And the spirit entered into me when he spake unto me, and set me upon my feet..."(Ezekiel 2:1-2).

The more obedient you are in yielding to those gentle tugs within, the more He can work His purpose out through you. You will live a great, miraculous life, if only the Spirit could take over your soul.
