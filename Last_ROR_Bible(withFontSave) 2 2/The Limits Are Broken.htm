PASTOR ANITA

THE LIMITS ARE BROKEN

When you received the Holy Spirit, you also received the anointing that removes burdens and destroys yokes. That anointing changes everything in your life. It guides you, leads you and destroys every limitation, such that you're able to face life with the giant-killer mentality. Your life as a Christian has been programmed in one direction only: upward and forward. No wonder the Bible says "...the path of the just is as the shining light, that shineth more and more unto the perfect day" (Proverbs 4:18). This is the path of your life; this is the road you've been called to travel. Refuse the up-and-down life; refuse sickness, poverty, failure and everything that's inconsistent with the glory-life you've been called to live. The Lord Jesus brought us a life of rest, peace, and fulfillment, where there are no limitations; you're at liberty to excel and live a glorious, purposeful and victorious life in Christ. Your life has been programmed to move from glory to glory and from faith to faith. Your tomorrow should be better than today, and your next year should be greater than this year. Remember, you've been brought into the glorious liberty of the sons of God; you're at liberty to succeed! Every hindrance in your life has been destroyed; the limits are off. Nothing can hold you back anymore.

"And it shall come to pass in that day, that his burden shall be taken away from off thy shoulder, and his yoke from off thy neck, and the yoke shall be destroyed because of the anointing" (Isaiah 10:27).

It's time to give a shout of victory to the Lord, for every wall of opposition has been knocked down, just as the walls of Jericho crumbled before the children of Israel.
