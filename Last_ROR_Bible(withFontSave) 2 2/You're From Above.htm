PASTOR CHRIS

YOU'RE FROM ABOVE

The most sublime and profound communication ever to proceed from anyone came from the lips of Jesus. The Jews in His day couldn't comprehend the wisdom and audacity with which He spoke. That's because Jesus communicated with a spiritual language, while they were men and women of the senses. For example, when He said in John 8:23, "...I am from above," He wasn't referring to a physical life. He was referring to a spiritual life! Jesus knew that His life didn't come from Mary, because He was the Word of God made flesh. Conscious of that reality, He could say with boldness that He was not of this world. Now, the Bible says concerning us that as He (Jesus) is, so are we in this world (1 John 4:17). If you're born again, you're no longer an ordinary person; you're from above just as Jesus, and you have the same indestructible life that He has. You've been born again of that same living Word that came from heaven (1 Peter 1:23), which means you too can declare like Jesus, "I am from above!" I would to God every believer in Christ will live with this consciousness. It will change everything about you and bring a supernatural perspective to your life! Don't think like the rest of the world; yes, you may look like them physically, but the life in you is from above. Train yourself in this consciousness. Speak a different language-the language of the Kingdom that you're from.

"And he said unto them, Ye are from beneath; I am from above: ye are of this world; I am not of this world" (John 8:23).

Refuse to speak sickness, disease, lack, and failure. They're not part of your heavenly Kingdom. I encourage you to carry this consciousness to work, school, and wherever you go.
