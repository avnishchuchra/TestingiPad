PASTOR CHRIS

A VERY PRESENT HELP

God is great, yet He is our friend and we can talk to Him as our companion. It's a relief to know that I'm never alone, not even for one moment! The Lord Jesus has already told us that in the last days "...there shall be signs in the sun, and in the moon, and in the stars; and upon the earth distress of nations, with perplexity; the sea and the waves roaring' (Luke 21:25). These are the pictures we see in the news on television, hear on radio and read in the newspapers daily. As Christians, irrespective of how vivid these pictures might be, they're not our portion. You must look away from all other things unto Jesus (Hebrews 12:2). If you observe the wind and the waves like Peter did, you'll most definitely sink (Matthew 14:26-31). The Bible says "Thou shalt not be afraid for the terror by night; nor for the arrow that flieth by day; Nor for the pestilence that walketh in darkness; nor for the destruction that wasteth at noonday. A thousand shall fall at thy side, and ten thousand at thy right hand; but it shall not come nigh thee. Only with thine eyes shalt thou behold and see the reward of the wicked" (Psalm 91:5-8). So you see, those negative reports you see, hear and read wouldn't come near your home, family or business as you remain in the Word of God.

"God is our refuge and strength, a very present in trouble' (Psalm 46:1).

Focus on good things and refuse to be moved by the devil's report because God is your refuge and strength, a tested help in times of trouble (Psalm 46:1).
