PASTOR CHRIS

THE DAYS OF THE LATTER RAIN

We're living in the days of the latter rain, the time of supernatural harvest and prosperity! These are the days the patriarchs and the prophets of old spoke of. The Lord Jesus called it "the days of great harvest." He said, "...The harvest truly is great, but the labourers are few..." (Luke 10:2). Indeed the labourers were few at the time. The Bible says, "Be patient therefore, brethren, unto the coming of the Lord. Behold, the husbandman waiteth for the precious fruit of the earth, and hath long patience for it, until he receive the early and latter rain" (James 5:7). There's a strong move of God's power in our time. The Lord has sent the former rain and the latter rain together (Joel 2:23), so we can reap an unprecedented harvest of souls with ease. You don't need to struggle to win souls anymore. Hallelujah! What's more, the former rain and the latter rain also bring financial and spiritual prosperity. Thus the same way the Word of God prevailed in Ephesus (Acts 19:20), the gospel of Christ is also growing and making tremendous impact in our day.

" Ask ye of the LORD rain in the time of the latter rain; so the LORD shall make bright clouds, and give them showers of rain, to every one grass in the field" (Zechariah 10:1).

There couldn't be a better time to declare the goodness of the Lord and the great things He's doing in our midst than now. We must thank the Lord for giving us the opportunity to reign, grow and increase at this time, for these are the days of His glory! Surely, it's the best time to be alive.
