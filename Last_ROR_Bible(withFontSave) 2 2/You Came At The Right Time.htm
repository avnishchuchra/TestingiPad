PASTOR CHRIS

YOU CAME AT THE RIGHT TIME

The Bible records that a wicked man by the name of Haman whipped up a conspiracy to destroy God's people who were in captivity. Being the Prime Minister, he was able to convince the king that it was necessary to annihilate them. But by this time, a young Jewish girl by the name of Esther had been chosen as the queen of the reigning king, Ahasuerus. When her uncle, Mordecai heard about it, he sent her the message in that she had come into the kingdom at this time for the purpose of saving God's people (Esther 4:13- 14.). At the risk of her life, Esther took up the challenge and Haman's plan backfired. She proved that she was born for such a time in the history of the Jews. Just like Esther, you were born for such a time as this. That means you came at the right time. Years ago, as a teenager walking with my little briefcase, I would say over and over again, "The world needs me! I was born as the answer to the cry of millions!" I knew that I came at the right time, for this generation, and my life has been programmed in that direction ever since. There's a reason you weren't born a century ago or a century from now; God purposed and planned that you would come in this generation, to be the answer to the questions of people in this generation.

" For if thou altogether holdest thy peace at this time, then shall there enlargement and deliverance arise to the Jews from another place; but thou and thy father's house shall be destroyed: and who knoweth whether thou art come to the kingdom for such a time as this?" (Esther 4:14).

You're not a mistake on the scene. God knew you were coming and He prepared for you. When you have this kind of mentality, you'll get out of the league of complainers and failures.
