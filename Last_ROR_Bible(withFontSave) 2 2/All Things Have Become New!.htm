PASTOR ANITA

ALL THINGS HAVE BECOME NEW!

There's a difference between the old "you" and the new "you" after you received Christ. The old "you" had the nature of sin, therefore he sinned at will. However, that old nature was crucified with Christ! Right now, you're a brand new person. This is because the new creation has no past; he never existed before. Hallelujah! As far as God is concerned, your past was buried with Jesus when He died for us. Your past failures and mistakes don't matter anymore. The day you were born again became the first day of your life. You can now chart your course for victory and success as you put the principles of God's Word to work. God has already determined a great future for the new creation; He is the God who declares the end from the beginning and "makes all things new" (Revelation 21:5). He has ordained everything in your life to go in the path of success. Refuse to judge yourself according to your past mistakes and manner of life, for "if any man be in Christ, he is a new creation: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new!' God has made all things in your life new; you're a brand new species of being. Refuse to allow the devil fill your mind with thoughts and memories of the sins you committed before you were born again, for as far as God is concerned, the handwriting of sin concerning you has been cancelled.

"Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new" (2 Corinthians 5:17).

A new life of righteousness and true holiness has begun for you.
