PASTOR CHRIS

TRUE JOY IS FROM WITHIN

For some people, they'll have to watch a comedy before they can laugh when they're feeling sad. However, the effect of this doesn't last, for it doesn't take long before all their troubles come rushing back to mind as soon as they're done with having a good laugh. Such people always find themselves unhappy without any reason, as they haven't learnt how to generate joy from within. Comedians cannot keep you happy permanently. They may make you laugh for a while but after the show is over, your situation remains unchanged. There is, however, a joy that comes from the inner man. This kind of joy transcends physical circumstances; it's permanent and will keep a man s t rong and al ive. In essence, true joy is the product of the recreated human spirit (Galatians 5:22). This means that if you're born again, joy is a part of you and God expects you to generate it from within your spirit. The Bible already told you that the joy of the Lord is your strength; that means if there's no joy, there'll be weakness in your life. You don't have to wait for anyone to make you happy; nobody has that responsibility. However, it's your responsibility to make others happy, for you can generate joy from within and spread it all around. The Bible says, "Delight thyself also in the LORD; and he shall give thee the desires of thine heart" (Psalm 37:4). Therefore, if you know how to generate joy from inside, you'll not even have to pray about everything you desire; God will take care of them all.

"...Go your way, eat the fat, and drink the sweet, and send portions unto them for whom nothing is prepared: for this day is holy unto our Lord: neither be ye sorry; for the joy of the LORD is your strength" (Nehemiah 8:10).

Learn to laugh at the devil and your troubles, and those challenges that seem insurmountable will suddenly fade away. So, go ahead and generate joy today!
