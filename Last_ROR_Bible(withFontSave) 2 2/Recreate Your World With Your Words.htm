PASTOR CHRIS

RECREATE YOUR WORLD
WITH YOUR WORDS

Do you have a situation that you want to change? Is there anything you want to make or see in your life? You don't have to cry about it. You can take the Word of God and use it to change things or recreate your world for the better. This is the principle that God used to create the world. The Bible says, "Through faith we understand that the worlds were framed by the word of God, so that things which are seen were not made of things which do appear" (Hebrews 11:3). The whole earth was a chaotic mass and full of darkness. The Holy Ghost was brooding over it, incubating upon it, but nothing changed. That period of incubation was important, because He was conceiving what He was about to create. Then in the midst of that chaos and darkness, God said, "Let there be light" and there was light! The Bible says concerning the Word of God that "All things were made by him; and without him was not any thing made that was made" (John 1:3). What do you want to create today? What situation do you want to change? Romans 10:8 says that the Word is near you and it's in your heart.

"And the earth was without form, and void; and darkness was upon the face of the deep. And the Spirit of God moved upon the face of the waters. And God said, Let there be light: and there was light" (Genesis 1:2-3).

That Word that created the universe and upholds it today is still here with us. Take it with you and lift your voice in prayer to God. When you have incubated upon that situation, go ahead and speak and recreate your world!
