PASTOR ANITA

A NEW MOVE

There's a new dimension of ministration going on in the realm of the spirit. It's a new day and people are coming into a deeper knowledge of God. And do you know what's going to happen? The former rain and the latter rain will be combined together in this last-day outpouring of the Spirit. Hallelujah! People in the world are going to experience such spiritual darkness as they have never seen before, and so the gospel is needed now more than ever. I tell you, if you have ever preached the gospel, now is the time. Double your efforts, preach it now more than ever. Let your zeal for the things of our Kingdom soar high. If they've ever called you a fanatic in your life, let it be at this time. It's time to walk in the visions of God; it's time to do the works of Jesus. The world is waiting for those that have the solution (Romans 8:19). In Science fiction they have talked about a new class of 'super' men. They said these men will come from outer space. What they don't know is that they're already down here on earth, and they're not green or blue or metallic, they are the Church of Jesus Christ. The world doesn't know it yet, but this is what they've been waiting for. The Church is going to receive God's knowledge like never before. We will exhibit such boldness that the world has never seen.

"Be glad then, ye children of Zion, and rejoice in the LORD your God: for he hath given you the former rain moderately, and he will cause to come down for you the rain, the former rain, and the latter rain in the first month" (Joel 2:23).

We will see even our little children preaching the gospel with boldness. There is a change in the realm of the Spirit. Don't wait; don't let it pass you by. Step right into the flow!
