PASTOR ANITA

YOU NEED SELF-CONTROL

The man who has no control over his spirit is like a city whose walls are broken down. This means he's vulnerable to attack and anything could bring him down. The devil could come into his life at any time and attack his family, his job, his business or his health. On the other hand, Proverbs 16:32 says, "...he that rules his spirit is better than he that takes a city." In other words, it's better to have self-control than to be a great warrior that doesn't have rule over his spirit. A Christian who has rule over his spirit is like a city fortified with protective walls that deter any invader. He is well covered and gives no room to the devil. He doesn't just receive anything that comes his way, or accept every word he's told. He doesn't do just anything his mind tells him to do; he has God's Word as his defense and standard. His spirit has gained the mastery over his mind and his body because he has been transformed through the renewing of his mind. Train your spirit through the Word; that way, you'll be able to decipher the good, the acceptable and the perfect will of God, and can choose the perfect will to walk in it (Romans 12:2). As you stay in the Word of God-hearing it, studying it, speaking it and doing it, your faith will be built, and you'll also be able to repel every dart the enemy throws at you. Your faith is your protective shield against all the wiles of the enemy, hence, the reason you must spend time building your faith-muscles.

" He that hath no rule over his own spirit is like a city that is broken down, and without walls" (Proverbs 25:28).

You need self-control because conquer ing your sel f would be your greatest conquest.
