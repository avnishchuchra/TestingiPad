PASTOR CHRIS

THE PRAYER OF AGREEMENT:
A SURE WAY OF PRAYING AND GETTING
RESULTS

The Bible teaches that there are different kinds of prayer. The prayer of agreement is one of them. It involves two or more people joining their faith together and deciding to pray concerning anything in particular that they desire. If, for instance, you want to effect a positive change in your job, family, health, or anything at all, that's when to call up someone who believes with you in the faith to pray in agreement with you. The prayer of agreement is a most sure way of praying and getting results. But be sure to follow the rules. Firstly, the scripture says if any two of you shall agree; that means it must involve two or more people. Then it says as touching anything that they shall ask; that means you must be specific. In other words, the parties involved in the prayer must be clear as to the exact request to make. Finally, you must pray in faith and in line with the Word. Having done all that, you can rest assured that your prayers would be answered. The Bible says, this is the confidence that we have in him, that, if we ask any thing according to his will, he heareth us" (1 John 5:14).

"And Again I say unto you, That if two of you shall agree on earth as touching any thing that they shall ask, it shall be done for them of my Father which is in heaven. For where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them" (Matthew 18:19-20).

The next time you want to effect a change concerning any situation, follow the rules of the prayer of agreement; it's one of the sure ways of praying and get t ing results.
