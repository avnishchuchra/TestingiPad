PASTOR CHRIS

LET THE WORD DOMINATE YOU!

The Word of God has the ability to impact not only your spirit and your mind but also your physical body. God's Word reaches right into your joints and bone marrow! That means as you take the Word into you, it goes first into your mind, then into your spirit. Then, as you continue to meditate on it, it goes right into your physical body, and impacts every fibre of your being. God's Word works in your spirit the same way physical food works in your body. Just as the food you eat is broken down and absorbed until it becomes part of you, so also the Word of God that you receive into you goes right into your spirit and becomes in you what it says. The Bible says, "Meditate upon these things; give thyself wholly to them; that thy profiting may appear to all" (1 Timothy 4:15). What things are you supposed to meditate on? God's Word! It says give yourself completely to the Word. That's another way of saying let God's Word dominate you. You have a responsibility to submit yourself to the Word of God and allow it inundate your entire being. Meditate on the Word to the point that it literally oozes out of your entire being-spirit, soul and body.

"For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword, piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart" (Hebrews 4:12 ).

Listen, study, and meditate on the scriptures until you can only think the Word way. Be a doer and not just a hearer of the Word. Have definite times when you listen to God's Word and study the Scriptures voraciously. Have definite times of meditation when you ruminate on the Word and speak it until it soaks deeply into you.
