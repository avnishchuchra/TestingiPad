PASTOR CHRIS

YOU NEED THE KNOWLEDGE OF
GOD'S WORD

When you buy a new electrical appliance, the first thing you ought to do is read the instruction manual in order to be informed on the proper use of the appliance. With this information, you're placed in a position of advantage as you'll be able to use that appliance properly. If, however, you're ignorant of how the appliance works, you'll not be able to use it to its maximum capacity. In the same vein, when you don't have knowledge of God's Word, you'll not be able to live in dominion and victory over sickness, poverty and the devil because you'll be ignorant of who you are, what you have and your rights in Christ. Proverbs 11:9 says, "...through knowledge shall the just be delivered." This scripture isn't referring to being delivered from the devil; it's talking about being delivered into your inheritance. Through the knowledge of God's Word, a Christian can live in dominion and victory over circumstances and situations; knowledge makes the Christian a freeman. In other words, knowledge is the key to your freedom. You have to realize it's only the knowledge of God's Word that'll move you forward in life. It'll instill in you the mentality of one who reigns and overcomes the world and its systems; the victor's mentality.

"My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge..." (Hosea 4:6).

There is no need to live a defeated life; it's unnecessary. Make up your mind today to learn God's Word. Don't be a prisoner of ignorance, rather, live in the freedom of knowledge.
