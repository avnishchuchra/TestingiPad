PASTOR ANITA

TAKE IT BY FORCE!

Whenever you pray or study God's Word on your own or in church together with other believers, you're invigorated spiritually. However, the true battle begins the moment you open your eyes or step out of the door of the church building. I'm talking life's everyday challenges: the old friends, habits, pains... financial crisis, which you must confront and ultimately conquer. But you can't win until you're conversant with the Word of God. As you grow in God's Word, you become awakened to the realities of God's provision for you through Christ Jesus. And through this knowledge, you live victoriously. If the knowledge you have of God's Word is insufficient, then you have unwittingly made yourself the devil's prime target. As you acquire more knowledge of God's Word, you must also learn to appropriate all that's written for your benefit into your life. However, you may not get whatever you desire just because it's written in the Bible. There are forces out there working to keep you from possessing those things which legally and rightfully belong to you in Christ. For this reason, you've got to be violent to possess your possession. The Lord Jesus said, "...the kingdom of heaven suffereth violence, and the violent take it by force" (Matthew 11:12). You've also got to fight the good fight of faith everyday (1 Timothy 6:12). Though the weapons of your warfare aren't physical, yet, they are mightily effective through God (2 Corinthians 10:4).

"But upon mount Zion shall be deliverance, and there shall be holiness; and the house of Jacob shall possess their possessions" (Obadiah 1:17).

Just as Abraham refused to consider his body or his wife's infertility so also you mustn't consider your situation but continually call yourself what God says you are in His Word. Refuse to stagger or vacillate. Stick with what the Word says for as long as it takes you to get what you want.
