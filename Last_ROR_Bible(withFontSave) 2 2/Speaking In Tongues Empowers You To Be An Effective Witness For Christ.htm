PASTOR CHRIS

SPEAKING IN TONGUES EMPOWERS
YOU TO BE AN EFFECTIVE WITNESS
FOR CHRIST

As God's people, we've been commissioned to preach the gospel and show forth the kingdomlife to the world. However, some people aren't functioning effectively in the ministry of reconciliation; they're not fervent soul winners! Such people allow opportunities to preach the gospel pass them by unutilized. They are those who make such comments as, "I feel shy whenever I want to preach to others." Well, not when you have the Holy Ghost, and definitely not when you make a habit of speaking in other tongues! One of the many benefits of speaking in tongues consistently is that it emboldens you to be an effective witness for Jesus Christ. I once heard a nine-year old boy testify, "I couldn't preach the Word of God to my classmates at school, but when I received the Holy Spirit and started speaking in tongues, I became bold. Then, each time I went to school, I preached to all my mates." The Apos t le Paul , a per fect example of an effective soul winner, gave us an inspiring insight to his success in missionary and evangelical work when he said, "...from Jerusalem, and round about unto Illyricum, I have fully preached the gospel of Christ" (Romans 15:19). The man was confident to state that he had fully preached the gospel from Jerusalem to Illyricum!

"But ye shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you: and ye shall be witnesses unto me both in Jerusalem, and in all Judaea, and in Samaria, and unto the uttermost part of the earth" (Acts 1:8).

He knew he had left no stone unturned; that's what it means to be an effective witness for Christ. But what was Paul's secret? Paul lets us in on one of the secrets that stood him out as an effective witness of Christ in 1 Corinthians 14:18, meaning he was a man who spoke in tongues very often and very intently, and that was key to his success in ministry. Practise speaking in other tongues always; it will energize your spirit to do the work of God.
