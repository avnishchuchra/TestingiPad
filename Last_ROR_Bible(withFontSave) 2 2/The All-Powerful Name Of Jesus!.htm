PASTOR CHRIS

THE ALL-POWERFUL
NAME OF JESUS!

The Bible tells us that Peter and John encountered a man who was lame from birth at the gate of the temple called Beautiful. Subsequently, when Peter said to the man, "In the Name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth, rise up and walk," life came into those dead limbs and the man leaped and walked! If Peter had shouted, "In the name of Caesar, rise up and walk!" nothing would have happened. If he had yelled as loud as he could, "In the name of Pontius Pilate rise up and walk,"nothing would have happened either. It had to be "In the Name of Jesus," for that's the Name above every name; that's the Name that unlocks every door! No matter the critical situation in which you've found yourself, you can use the Name of Jesus to effect a change. It may be impossible for you on your own to get out of that negative situation, just like the crippled man at the gate couldn't walk by his own ability, but through the power in the Name of Jesus, you can walk out of any trouble. All you need do is call that Name in faith and you'll walk out of poverty, sickness, disease, depression and failure. The Name of Jesus is the Name you can trust; it's the Name that never fails! By the power of that Name, I prophesy to you today: you're walking out of that difficulty.

"Then Peter said, Silver and gold have I none; but such as I have give I thee: In the name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth rise up and walk. And he took him by the right hand, and lifted him up: and immediately his feet and ankle bones received strength. And he leaping up stood, and walked, and entered with them into the temple, walking, and leaping, and praising God" (Acts 3:6-8).

If you've been sick, I pronounce you healed! If you couldn't walk, begin to walk! I declare success, promotion, increase and prosperity for you in the mighty Name of Jesus! Whatever you couldn't do before, do it now, in the all-powerful Name of Jesus!
