PASTOR CHRIS

A PACKAGE OF DIVINE POWER!

When you're born again, your spirit becomes infused with the presence and power of God. You become a divine package of power. As a Christian, never think you're an ordinary person; you're peculiar! You're superior to satan, poverty, failure sickness, disease, infirmities and death. You're not the "poor, old, weakling" some around you may think you are. You're an embodiment of divine power, a bundle of success, and an epithet of prosperity. It's impossible for anything to put you down in life. You've been anointed with the Holy Ghost and with power to tame the adversary. The Bible says "ye shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you" (Acts 1:8). You've been endued with supernatural power. You can change any negative situation. You can completely shut the devil up, and permanently stop his maneuvers and manipulations in your life and in the lives of your loved ones. No devil, sickness or disease can stay in your body; the power of God in you will burn them up. Get to know who you are; you're a supernatural being! Never be afraid of anything. The Bible says "NOTHING shall by any means hurt you," absolutely nothing! (Luke 10:19)! Why? Because you're a divine package of power! Remember Jesus; when the Roman soldiers came to arrest Him, He asked them "Whom do you seek?" They said "Jesus of Nazareth." All He said was, "I am he" and the power of God hit them and they all went backwards and fell to the ground (John 18:4-6).

"But truly I am full of power by the spirit of the LORD, and of judgment, and of might..." (Micah 3:8).

You too are loaded with as much power. That power is s ickness -destroying, demon-chasing, failureproof and poverty-resistant.
