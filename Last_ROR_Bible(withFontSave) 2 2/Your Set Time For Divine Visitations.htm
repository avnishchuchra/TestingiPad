YOUR SET TIME FOR
DIVINE VISITATIONS

The Scriptures contain several accounts of men and women who experienced divine visitations. Sometimes it was a visit by an angel; at other times it was the Lord Himself. These visitations were always lifechanging, destiny-altering experiences. The Bible tells us about Abraham who, on a certain day, sat in his tent door in the heat of the day and saw three men passing by. By faith, he perceived they weren't ordinary men and called out to them to stop over for some refreshments (Genesis 18:1-5). It turned out that one of the three men was the Lord Himself, while the other two were angels. But guess what? It was during that encounter that the Lord told Abraham exactly when Sarah would conceive and give birth (Genesis 18:10). When Jacob was at a crossroads in his life, it took a divine visitation from the Lord for him to experience his much desired turnaround. He was alone one day when an angel came to him and wrestled with him all night long (Genesis 32:24). At the break of dawn, he realized the angel wasn't an ordinary angel but the Spirit of God Himself, and pleaded with Him to bless him. That was the day the Lord changed his name from "Jacob" which meant "usurper" or "swindler" to "Israel," which means "a prince of power with God." That divine visitation changed his destiny and set him on the path of greatness. We're in the last days, and God wants to visit you and touch your life in a special way.

"Be not forgetful to entertain strangers: for thereby some have entertained angels unawares" (Hebrews 13:2).

Divine visitations were not reserved only for people in the Old Testament. So you can also expect to be divinely visited by God. A divine encounter with the Lord will definitely change your life forever.
