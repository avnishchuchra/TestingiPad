PASTOR CHRIS

HOW TO ENCOURAGE
THE MINISTRY OF ANGELS

As Christians, we're surrounded by myriads of angels, and the Bible says they are ministering spirits sent to minister for us. That means they're at our service even though we don't see them with our physical eyes. Some people are more conscious of demons than they are of angels and that's sad. The truth is there're far more angels than demons, which is why you must learn to encourage the ministry of angels in your life. In encouraging the ministry of angels in your life, first you must recognize that angels are real and they're ever-present with you. When I was just getting introduced to their ministry years ago, if I needed to be woken up very early to pray, I'd pray a simple prayer, asking the Lord to wake me. Sure enough, I'd feel a tap on my arm and as soon as I opened my eyes, the clock would read the exact time I wanted to wake up. At other times, I'd hear an audible knock at my door at a time when the whole house was fast asleep. When I opened the door, there'd be nobody there. The Lord was introducing me to the ministry of angels, and I've been conscious of their presence with me ever since. As you act on the Word of God and confess it rightly, you're encouraging the ministry of angels in your life. Angels help to bring to pass the Word of God that you speak. When you speak negative confessions of doubt and unbelief, they can't act on your behalf. You also encourage the ministry of angels in your life when you learn to be subject to spiritual authority.

"But to which of the angels said he at any time, Sit on my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool? Are they not all ministering spirits, sent forth to minister for them who shall be heirs of salvation?" (Hebrews 1:13-14).

So stay in the Word of God and make your confessions of faith. When you have supernatural experiences like the examples I gave, don't dismiss them, God's angels are working with you.
