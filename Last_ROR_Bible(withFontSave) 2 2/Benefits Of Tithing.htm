PASTOR CHRIS

BENEFITS OF TITHING

The tithe is one-tenth of your increase; that is, ten percent of your income and anything else God blesses you with. For example, if you receive $7,000 as your pay or as a gift, ten percent of that amount, which is $700, is your tithe. That $700 belongs to God. One of the surest ways to experience prosperity in God's kingdom is through tithing. It's surer than saving money in the bank because of the promise attached to it (Malachi 3:10). Your tithe is your connection to your source of blessing. It doesn't matter how much you've got now, if you don't tithe, it's impossible for you to continue in prosperity. But as long as that connection is maintained, there will be an unending flow of supernatural prosperity. If you own a business, you should be smart enough to connect it to your source of blessing through your tithe. If you don't, you'll experience a lot of struggling and the business will eventually fade. God is the source of wealth, and He wants you to connect your job or business to Him through your tithe.

"Bring ye all the tithes into the storehouse, that there may be meat in mine house, and prove me now herewith, saith the LORD of host, if I will not open you the windows of heaven, and pour you out a blessing, that there shall not be room enough to receive it" (Malachi 3:10).

Connect with Him from the onset, even when your business is still small or it seems like things aren't working out so well. That connection is proof things are going to work out, and you'll be a great success. When you tithe, your job or business and your finances are protected. God rebukes the devourer for your sake, and instead of losses, there's a blessing upon you and you find yourself riding higher and higher in permanent prosperity.
