PASTOR CHRIS

YOUR CHANNEL FOR WEALTH

The story of Elijah and the widow of Zarephath in 1 Kings 17 is a touching one. The widow gave the prophet of God all she had, which was a ration that was just enough to keep herself and her son alive. The woman's faith moved Elijah to proclaim blessings of prosperity upon her (1 Kings 17:14). The little she had was multiplied and she didn't lack throughout the duration of the famine. If the widow and her son, had eaten the food, they would have died. But she was smart enough to recognize that true prosperity comes from the Lord. She acted her faith and gave all she had to the man of God and she got her miracle. When you study your Bible, you would discover that the kings and governments of the day weren't responsible for the prosperity of the people. The prophets were the ones responsible, for they were God's representatives. Not much has changed since then. Crying to political leaders to improve the economy of the nation wouldn't yield much result because you're calling on the wrong people. The power to get wealth is not with the kings, the politicians or the governments but the Lord (Deuteronomy 8:18). In 2 King 6:26, a woman cried out to the king for help as there was severe famine in the land. The king responded quickly, "...If the LORD do not help thee, whence shall I help thee?" (2 Kings 6:27). Even the king knew he couldn't do anything about the devastated economy of the country without God's help.

"For thus saith the LORD God of Israel, The barrel of meal shall not waste, neither shall the cruse of oil fail, until the day that the Lord sendeth rain upon the earth" ( 1 Kings 17:14 ).

You have to realize that God wants you prosperous and He has sent your man of God to bless you. Therefore, when the land is dry, don't look up to the government or the politicians to help you, rather, look unto God.
