PASTOR ANITA

HIS MERCIES ARE NEW
EVERY MORNING!

The Bible lets us know that the manna with which God fed the children of Israel in the wilderness couldn't be eaten stale. It was always fresh. In the same way, God's tender mercies for us are new every morning. He's always watching you, guiding you and protecting you. He is gracious towards you. God's mercy is so strong. The Bible says He's rich in mercy and loves us infinitely (Ephesians 2:4). To the world, God is judgment, but to us His kids, He is love. He said in Jeremiah 31:3, "Yea, I have loved thee with an everlasting love: therefore with lovingkindness have I drawn thee." No wonder He tells us to approach His throne of grace with boldness and obtain mercy, and find grace to help in time of need (Hebrews 4:16). What joy! What comfort; to know that God is not angry with me! Instead, He renews His love and mercies towards me every morning! Take advantage of the grace and mercies of God. Don't condemn yourself because of what you did wrong, for He never condemns you: "There is therefore now no condemnation to them which are in Christ Jesus..." (Romans 8:1).

" It is of the LORD'S mercies that we are not consumed, because his compassions fail not. They are new every morning: great is thy faithfulness" (Lamentations 3:22- 23).

He sees the best in you always, and expects you to see the best in yourself and others around you as well.
